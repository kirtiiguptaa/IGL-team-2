Amazing Hammer

Prep: 10 mins

Serves: 1

CONFECTION

Squeeze the juice from 1 large or 2 small oranges and 1 clementine and set aside. Put a handful of ice cubes into a tall glass and pour over 50ml Amazing Vodka followed by the fruit juices. Stir gently to combine then add a few dashes of Spearmint and a wedge of orange to garnish

Amazing Lemon Drop

Prep: 10 mins

Serves: 1

CONFECTION

Put 50 ml Amazing Vodka in a tall glass in the fridge to chill. Mix 1 tablespoon chat masala with the finely grated zest of tiny chipped ginger, set aside. Pour chilled 50ml Amazing Vodka into a cocktail shaker and add 25ml lemon juice and a handful of ice. Shake really well until the outside of the shaker feels cold add a few dashes of coriander to garnish. Strain the contents of the shaker into your prepared glass and enjoy.

Amazing Sea breeze

Prep: 10 mins

Serves: 1

CONFECTION

Half-fill a tall glass with ice then pour in 50ml Amazing Vodka, 100ml cranberry juice and 50ml grapefruit juice. Stir gently until the outside of the glass feels cold then garnish with a thin slice of lime.

Amazing Mojito

Prep: 10 mins

Serves: 1

CONFECTION

Muddle mint leaves, lime and simple syrup in a highball glass. Fill with crushed ice. Add Amazing Vodka. Stir. Top up with soda water. Garnish with mint leaf.

Zumba Black Mojito

Prep: 10 mins

Serves: 1

CONFECTION

1. Take the lime wedges and squeeze them in the glass. Gently press together the limes and sugar.

2. Add mint to glass, and press with bar spoon to release oil. half fill the glass with crushed ice, add 50 ml ZUMBA black rum & churn. Top with crushed ice and soda. Garnish with 2 or 3 leaves of mint.

Zumba Rum with Cola

Prep: 10 mins

Serves: 1

CONFECTION

Simple add 50 ml of ZUMBA black rum and add crushed ice and top the glass with cola. To give it a tingy touch add few drops of lemon juice.
