About IGL

IGL has a global presence and partners in various countries, which makes us an inclusive and diverse enterprise. With an impressive portfolio of pharmaceutical, food and beverage, personal care and industrial sector products, we touch lives every day, everywhere.

Our Mission

  • Provide high-quality and sustainable solutions to meet the diverse needs of our customers
  • Evolve and improve the efficiency and effectiveness of our operations
  • Ensure the highest levels of customer satisfaction
  • Create value for all our stakeholders through ethical and transparent business practices

Our Vision

  • To be the global leader in sustainable and innovative solutions
  • To touch the lives of our customers and exceed their expectations
  • Be a responsible corporate citizen and contribute towards the well-being of diverse communities and society
  • To stay at the forefront of the bio-based chemicals industry and create value for all our stakeholders
