From every atom of our products to every heartbeat of our workforce, we stand for being green in our thoughts, values and actions.

We are India's leading green chemical company. We offer the finest performance chemicals such as Glycols, Glycol Ethers, Acetates, Bio-Polymers, Natural Gums, and Potable Alcohol that have end applications across businesses and industries. Inspired by nature and chemistry, we create compounds based on decades of expertise, innovative engineering and sustainable technologies that touch millions of lives every day while ensuring our environment remains untouched.
