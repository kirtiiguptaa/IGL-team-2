Inspired by Nature. Enabled by Science. Created by Us.

Nature, Technology, and Innovation are the foundations on which Ennature Biopharma, the Natural Ingredients Division of IGL is built.

Our line of products is plant-based, developed by scientific experts, and manufactured in state-of-art facilities located in Dehradun, India. We provide high-quality, differentiated solutions for the pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and food and beverage industries.

Saving the planet. One innovation at a time.

We strongly believe in being ‘Green’ and integrate our pioneering green technologies into every aspect of our processes. From using SCF-CO2 extraction technology and ‘Green’ solvent-based extraction to reusing our raw materials for energy generation and water conservation, we are truly committed to leading the way with our green initiatives. We strive to build sustainable supply chains and we are continuously improving our technologies to conserve natural resources and reduce our carbon footprint.
