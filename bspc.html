Created by science. Crafted with passion.

The tenets of IGL are Human ingenuity, Innovative technology, sustainability and Green chemistry. These are the tenets of IGL. We set the highest standards and benchmarks in the Chemicals Industry with a team of experts that put their limitless capabilities into every solution.

Production and quality

IGL has future-ready research and development facilities where its team of experts work on the development of new products and improvement of existing ones. The company also has industry-leading certifications across its manufacturing facilities.

Reliability

IGL is a leading manufacturer of bio glycols , bio glycols ether & it's derivatives and performance chemicals in India. It has a strong track record of financial performance and is well-respected in the industry for the quality and reliability of its products. The company has a long history of operation and has established itself as a reliable partner for its customers and stakeholders.

Research and Development

The company has a dedicated research and development (R&D) team that works on a variety of projects, including developing new products and technologies, improving existing products, and finding more efficient and sustainable production methods. Some of the areas of research and development that IGL is currently focusing on include renewable energy, biotechnology, and specialty chemicals.

Sustainability

The company is committed to reducing its environmental impact and promoting sustainable practices in all aspects of its operations. We have also implemented energy-efficient practices in its manufacturing processes, which helps to reduce energy consumption and further reduce its carbon footprint.

safety

IGL is dedicated to ensuring the health and safety of its employees and the communities in which it operates, as well as protecting the environment. Some of the measures that we have undertaken are implementing safe work practices and procedures to prevent accidents and injuries, providing training to employees on HSE issues and emergency response and monitoring environmental performance, management and pollution.

CSR

CSR initiatives at IGL aim to contribute to the well-being and development of communities and the country as a whole. Our initiatives focus on empowering local workforce, improving healthcare, enhancing education, and building awareness about sustainability in the long-term.

Logistics

We offer end-to-end logistics solutions. Whether it's the movement of raw materials, finished goods, or anything in between, we have the expertise and resources to get the job done efficiently and cost-effectively. Our state-of-the-art logistics management system ensures that your goods are tracked and traced at every stage of the journey, giving you complete visibility and control.
