Sustainability vision

You. Me. We.
Nature is our responsibility.

The change starts with one.

Being a responsible and sustainable citizen means taking actions that prioritize the well-being of the planet and its inhabitants. This includes reducing our carbon footprint, conserving resources, and supporting environmentally-conscious initiatives and policies.

Company goal

Business Excellence

IGL is committed to balancing business excellence with environmental responsibility. From its very inception, the company has implemented eco-friendly technologies, focused on responsible sourcing with a robust waste management system, and reduced greenhouse gas emissions. As we look ahead, we intend to improve energy efficiency, develop sustainable supply chains, and implement waste management practices. Through these initiatives, the company is showing its leadership in driving positive change for the environment while maintaining its business excellence.

Company goal

Customer Satisfaction

As part of our commitment to customer satisfaction, we are focused on reducing our carbon footprint and making eco-friendly products that help build, support, and sustain community development projects. The company is dedicated to balancing economic growth for our customers with environmental protection and social responsibility for communities. Through these initiatives, we are aiming to create a better future for our customers, employees, and the environment.

Company goal

Environment Stewardship

The company has adopted sustainable manufacturing processes that minimize the use of harmful chemicals and promote the use of environmentally-friendly alternatives. Additionally, the company has invested in research and development of new technologies to minimize the impact of its operations on the environment. Moreover, we have promoted a culture of embracing nature and spreading awareness among our employees and stakeholders to enhance our reputation as a responsible corporate citizen.

Creating a sustainable environment

Our values

Ethical. Responsible. Accountable.

These are core values at IGL to build a strong, trustworthy brand. These values demonstrate a commitment to doing what is right, being transparent and trustable for our actions, and taking a proactive approach towards building a better future. By adhering to these values, we can establish ourselves as reliable sources of information, foster positive relationships with our stakeholders, and set an example for others to follow.

No compromise on safety and sustainability.

Keeping our employees, communities, and the environment safe and sustainable is crucial. We adhere to strict safety protocols and implement sustainable practices in our operations to demonstrate our commitment to social responsibility. We are continuously striving to enhance safety and sustainability standards to build trust and confidence with our customers, stakeholders, and the public.

Empower the workforce with the power of well-being

In order to maintain a productive and positive work environment, IGL invests in the well-being of its employees. Employee-centered initiatives demonstrate a commitment to valuing, supporting, and promoting the health and happiness of employees. These steps enhance employee satisfaction, increase productivity, and foster a culture of positivity and teamwork.

Respect. Respond. Report. All stakeholders.

The key to building a strong and lasting relationship is to listen to the concerns and feedback of our customers, employees, communities, and investors. By demonstrating transparency, accountability, and responsiveness, IGL can enhance its reputation as a responsible and trustworthy corporate citizen, and nurture positive relationships with its stakeholders.

The right chemistry of inclusivity and diversity.

As a chemical company, IGL promotes and protects human rights as a core value. This includes respecting the rights of employees, communities, and other stakeholders, and taking action to prevent human rights abuses in its supply chains. We are committed to following ethical business practices, social responsibility, and building a better future for all. Through its actions and policies, IGL sets a positive example for other companies to follow..p>

Protecting business profits and the environment

The company has implemented several sustainable practices, technologies, and several energy-efficient practices. Through these initiatives, the company aims to create a harmonious balance between economic growth and environmental conservation to inspire other businesses to prioritize sustainability in their operations.p>
